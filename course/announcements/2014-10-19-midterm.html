Midterm
Posted:Oct 19, 2014 at 11:00 PM

I wrote the midterm this weekend. It consists of 77 multiple choice questions, and 2 (very) short answer questions. Of the multiple choice, 20 are true/false, and a few are multiple-multiples.

The exam took me 27 minutes to finish (and I was carefully reading each question as a student taking the exam would). Even if takes you three times as long, you should still have about 20 minutes to check over your work at the end. All multiple choice questions are equally weighted (1 mark each), so pace yourself at one question/minute, and move on if you are stuck on a question.

The questions cover every section from 1.1 - 3.4, as well as labs 1 and 3. If you’ve been coming to class and following the material, I think you’ll find the exam quite doable.
